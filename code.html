                            <dt><abbr title="Hypertext Markup Language">HTML</abbr></dt>
                            <dd>
                              The basic <mark>structure</mark> of the house. So, constructing the foundation walls, perhaps a basement
                              and installing doors, windows and the roof.
                            </dd>
                            <dt><abbr title="Cascading Style Sheets">CSS</abbr></dt>
                            <dd>
                              The <mark>look</mark> of the house. So, painting the walls in nice colors, turning the roof red and
                              painting the windows and doors with a light wood finish.
                            </dd>
                            <dt><abbr title="JavaScript">JS</abbr></dt>
                            <dd>
                              The <mark>functionality</mark> of the house. So, installing a doorbell, laying electricity and water
                              pipes and installing all the appliances such as telephones, TVs or the kitchen stove.
                            </dd>
                          </dl>
                        </div>
